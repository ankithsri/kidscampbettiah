About Us

Kids Camp Bettiah nurtures young minds with joyful learning, creativity, and fun activities in a safe environment. We focus on overall development of children through play, arts, and structured learning activities.

Our Mission

To create a safe, happy, and stimulating environment where every child can explore, learn, and grow.

Our Vision

To be the leading preschool in Bettiah that inspires lifelong learning and confidence in every child.
